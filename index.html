Welcome to Antigua's Garden of Eden, the best place to stay in Antigua, Guatemala. We, Juan & Mercedes, are delighted to be your hosts. Nestled between 5 and 10 minute walk from Antigua's center, our beautiful Airbnb has been a haven for countless guests seeking a tranquil retreat.

With years of experience in hospitality, we take pride in creating an atmosphere where every guest feels truly welcomed. As locals from Chimaltenango, we have a deep connection to this region and are excited to share its wonders with you.

Our guesthouse offers a serene escape from the bustling streets of Antigua. Yet, you'll find yourself just a short walking distance away from Antigua's best bars, restaurants, and market. Indulge in the vibrant local cuisine, savor a drink at the finest establishments, and immerse yourself in the lively atmosphere.

After an exciting day of exploring Antigua or embarking on a breathtaking volcano hike, our peaceful oasis awaits your return. Relax amidst lush gardens, surrounded by the beauty of nature. Take a moment to rejuvenate your senses and find solace in the tranquility this beautiful hotel provides.

At Antigua's Garden of Eden, we are committed to making your stay exceptional. Our passion for hospitality drives us to ensure your comfort and satisfaction. If you have any questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. Your happiness and enjoyment are our top priorities.
